# Walking with Moms in Need

## Description
Walking with Moms in Need is a church ministry registry that helps a support group coordinator manage a live list of items needed by new and expecting moms, and allows congregation members to claim and donate those items without needing to create an account. It works like a gift registry but for an ever-changing list of needs rather than a one-time event.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: No Central Place to See What's Needed
Without a shared list, donors rely on word-of-mouth and often don't know what's actually needed at any given time. Walking with Moms in Need gives the coordinator a single place to maintain a live needs list that donors can check at any time.

### Problem 2: Duplicate Donations
When multiple donors give the same item, some needs go unmet while others are over-supplied. Item claiming marks an item as committed the moment a donor signs up, so others know to choose something else.

### Problem 3: Coordinator Has No Visibility into Fulfillment
Without a tracking system, the coordinator has no way to know which items have been claimed, who claimed them, or what still needs attention. The commitments dashboard gives the coordinator a clear view of all donor pledges and item status.

### Problem 4: Donors Have No Confirmation of Their Commitment
Donors who give their name and email with no follow-up may forget or feel uncertain about what they signed up for. Email confirmations reinforce the commitment and provide delivery details.

## Key Features
- Public registry page donors can visit without logging in
- Coordinator admin for managing the needs list
- Item claiming: donors enter name and email to commit to a specific item
- Quantity tracking so partially-filled needs remain visible
- Brand and store preferences captured per item
- Email confirmation sent to donor on claim
- Coordinator notified by email when an item is claimed
- Commitments dashboard for tracking fulfillment status
